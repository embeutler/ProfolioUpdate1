My Tech Story...

It all started Christmas Morning 1989 me and my little brother would wake up to a life changing gift eventhough we wouldn't know it at the time. Santa Clause had given us Prodigy it was a disk that would allow us to connect to people all over the world. At the time I had no clue just what the web would bring and the amount of time I would spend on "bulletin boards". Fastforward a few years to senior year of highschool (1998) I began to learn HTMLl and build my 1st website.

After returning from a study abroad progam in Italy (art history) I had the urge to start building again, so I decided to signed up for a web design class at the local JC. From there I would build a few more sites for friends and then move into a new job of helping create an online reference database for a sound distrubuter. After that project ended in the early 2000's I would move on to the telecom industry.

Shortly after starting my career in the telecom industry as a phone rep, I was hired to work within the finance department on a prohject for automating internal processes and document retention. From there I would assist creating a database that would allow for offline reps to track work then intergrate it into the company wide billing systems.

When those projects ended I moved on to work on a reference content project that would take all the company policies and procedures throuh a complete overhaul and began creating smart tools that would integrate company polices amd procedure within company specific systems used by reps through out the company. In this project I server as one of the main project managers for all rewrites of policies and procedures working with various teams throught out the company.

This project lead into the creation of smart guides that utilize iLog (busiess rule engine) and allowed for proceses to become automated for customers. The project resulted in reducing company overhead by $25 million a year with in customer contacts centers.
